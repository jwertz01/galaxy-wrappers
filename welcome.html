Information specific to this instance of Galaxy
  1. This link has details specific to this installation of Galaxy.
  2. A list of tools installed on this instance of Galaxy is available at this link.
  3. Do not upload, analyze or store data containing Personally Identifiable Information (PII), Personal Health Information (PHI), or data subject to state or federal data privacy and protection regulations.
  4. Data uploaded to or generated by this instance is not backed up. Please save your data to another location.
  5. Users consuming more than 500 gigabytes of storage space will be charged $22.50 per terabyte per quarter based on average storage utilization over a 3 month period.
  6. Data associated with user accounts exceeding two years of inactivity is subject to deletion.
